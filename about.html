OUR STORY

A Legacy of Quality

Building Dreams Since 2008

Founded in Vadodara, Aqua Enterprise began with a simple mission: to elevate the standard of aquatic construction in India. Over the years, we have grown from a small passionate team into one of Gujarat's most trusted names in luxury pool design and engineering.

Our approach combines state-of-the-art technology with time-honored craftsmanship. We believe that a pool is not just a body of water, but a centerpiece of living—a place where memories are made, relaxation is found, and architecture meets nature.

"We don't just build pools; we craft environments that inspire and endure for generations to come."

LEADERSHIP

Vikram Sharma

His visionary approach has led the company to win numerous industry awards and set new benchmarks for quality in the region.
