Our Construction Process

From Ground to Grandeur

Phase 01

Excavation

The pool area is marked and carefully excavated to shape the design.

This is the foundation stage where the structure begins to take form.

Phase 02

Steel Framework Setup

A strong steel reinforcement grid is installed across the base and walls.

This framework gives the pool its strength and long-term durability.

Phase 03

Structural Framing Refinement

The reinforcement is adjusted and detailed to match the final pool shape.

Every curve and level is prepared before concrete work begins.

Phase 04

Concrete Shell Formation

Concrete is applied to create the solid shell of the pool.

Now the pool's actual structure becomes clearly visible.

Phase 05

Finishing & Edge Detailing

The surface is smoothed and coping (top edge) is installed.

This step gives the pool a clean, refined look.

Phase 06

Tile Installation

Premium tiles are laid to enhance aesthetics and waterproofing.

The pool starts to come alive with color and texture.

Phase 07

Final Pool & Deck Completion

Water is filled and the surrounding deck is completed beautifully.

The space transforms into a ready-to-use luxury pool area.
